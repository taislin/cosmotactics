QUICK START GUIDE

Welcome, Commander! This guide will get you into the action immediately. For detailed rules and unit data, consult the Full Operator's Manual.

1. Your Objective

  • Your mission: Explore alien planets and find the **Stairs Down (>)** to proceed to the next level.
  • Keep your **Oxygen** supply above zero!
  • Eliminate hostile alien lifeforms.

2. Basic Controls

  • **Movement:** Use WASD (or Numpad) to move your selected operative one tile.
  • **Action:** Press Enter or Space to confirm moves or fire at a target.
  • **Change Unit:** Press N to cycle through your available squad members.
  • **Interact:** Press U to pick up items or use the stairs.
  • **Pass Turn:** Press T if your selected operative has no action.

3. Combat Essentials

  • **Simultaneous Turns:** When you move or fire with your selected unit, *all* other units (friendly AI and enemies) act at the same time!
  • **Firing:** Press F to enter **Fire Mode**. Aim with arrow keys, then Enter to shoot.
  • **Melee:** If you move adjacent to an enemy while in **Move Mode**, your operative will automatically engage in melee.
  • **Squad AI:** Your other squad members act autonomously based on their **Stance**.
    • P: Toggle **Stance** (Follow / Hold).
    • O: Toggle **Autofire** (On / Off).

4. Important UI Elements

  • **Game Map (Left):** Your view of the world. Bright areas are currently visible. Dim areas have been explored.
  • **Info Panel (Right):** Displays selected unit's stats, game log, and equipment.
  • **Health:** (Red Bar) Your unit's HP.
  • **Oxygen:** (Green Bar) Shared squad resource. Depletes each turn. Collect Oxygen Tanks!
